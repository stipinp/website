SURI DIMENSION

A long-term archaeological-digital graphic project.

SURI DIMENSION emerges from over thirteen years of archaeological and visual research into the iconographies of Northwest Argentina (NOA). At its core, it is a design-based study of a territory marked by extraordinary symbolic, archaeological, and geological richness—yet whose cultural traditions remain largely unexplored.

My earliest approaches began with clay, mud, and ceramics, later evolving into drawing. Today, this research continues through pixel art, used as a method of digital synthesis to develop cultural and heritage identity within interactive and game-based environments.

Suri Dimension

An evolving system of pixel-based environments, artifacts and speculative structures.The game is only one excavation layer among many.

Rooted in the iconographies and landscapes of Northwest Argentina, Suri Dimension reactivates archaeological memory through contemporary digital forms. +

StipinPixel is a visual artist working with pixel systems, digital archaeology, speculative interfaces and game-based environments.

Designer, artist, curator, educator and game developer, working across physical and digital contexts.

His practice connects local heritage and global digital cultures through pixel art, games, generative systems and collaborative projects focused on cultural memory and experimental formats.
